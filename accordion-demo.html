🔧 Installation Instructions

Prerequisites:

  • Unlocked bootloader
  • Custom recovery (TWRP recommended)
  • Full data backup

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Download the PixelOS ZIP file from the link below
  2. Boot into TWRP recovery (Volume Down + Power)
  3. Wipe data, cache, and system partitions
  4. Flash the PixelOS ZIP file
  5. (Optional) Flash GAPPS for Google services
  6. Reboot to system and enjoy!
⚠️ Note: Make sure to take a backup before proceeding. This process will wipe your data.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is this ROM stable for daily use?
A: Yes! PixelOS is known for its stability and smoothness.

Q: Will I lose my data during installation?
A: Yes, the installation process requires a factory reset. Make sure to backup your data.

Q: How do I update to newer versions?
A: You can dirty flash the new build over the old one. No need to wipe data.

Q: Does it support GCAM?
A: Yes! PixelOS comes with GCAM support out of the box.
